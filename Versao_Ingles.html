objective

The purpose of its website is to provide resources and information to help people who are blind and have low vision to develop their skills and increase their independence. Our site provides the best tools and techniques to help with reading, writing, browsing the web , as well as communication and other daily activities.

The site aims to help its target audience to overcome barriers and challenges that they may encounter in their daily lives and to develop skills for greater autonomy.

Tools

Accessibility tools are programs and technologies that help visually impaired people interact with computers, smartphones and other devices. Some of these tools include screen readers and magnifiers, assistive input and reading technologies, and web accessibility techniques.

These tools are essential to allow these people to access information and communicate digitally.
